How to Play

Use ARROW KEYS or W/S keys to move the paddle up and down.

Use A/D keys or LEFT/RIGHT arrow keys to move the paddle left and right.

Moving the paddle faster when hitting the ball will make it shoot off faster!

Move quickly in the opposite direction to hit the ball behind your paddle!

Press SPACEBAR to create a boost field in front of your paddle that accelerates the ball when it passes through! (3-second cooldown)

Player 1: Use W/S keys to move the left paddle up/down and A/D keys to move left/right.

Player 2: Use ARROW UP/DOWN keys to move the right paddle up/down and LEFT/RIGHT keys to move left/right.

Moving paddles faster when hitting the ball will make it shoot off faster!

Move quickly in the opposite direction to hit the ball behind your paddle!

Player 1: Press SHIFT to create a boost field | Player 2: Press SPACEBAR to create a boost field!

When the ball passes through a portal, it enters a new dimension with different physics!

Each dimension has unique visual effects and gameplay mechanics.
